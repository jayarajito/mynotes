Probes in Kubernetes
---------------------

A Pod is restarted automatically by kubernetes if k8s find the pod is unhealthy

this check health of the main process of the pod

k8s does not check the health of the other process(interal application)

for some reason if restart the container application works

inthis case how to know k8 needs to restart the container

Probes:
Investigates the pods if they are working correctly or not
